## 🚨 Common Issues & Solutions

### "No one is visiting my site"
**Solution**: 
- Post daily on Twitter/X
- Answer questions on Reddit (provide value first)
- Write blog posts (SEO takes time)
- Reach out directly to potential customers

### "People visit but don't sign up"
**Solution**:
- Add more social proof (testimonials, case studies)
- Offer free security scan
- Reduce friction (social login)
- Add live chat

### "People sign up but don't pay"
**Solution**:
- Follow up personally (email/call)
- Offer free consultation
- Provide case study showing ROI
- Offer discount for first customers

### "Payment processing fails"
**Solution**:
- Check Stripe keys (test vs live)
- Verify webhook is configured
- Check Railway logs for errors
- Test with Stripe test card first
